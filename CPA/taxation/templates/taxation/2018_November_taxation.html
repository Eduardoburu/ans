(c) Bandika Ltd., a company dealing in a variety of value added tax (VAT) designated goods, was registered for VATS purposes on 1 March 2018.

The following transactions were recorded for the month of March 2018:

  1. March 10: Opening stock 9,200 units valued at Sh.85 per unit.
  2. March 5: Imported 10,000 units at Sh.80 per unit being cost, insurance and freight (CIF).
  3. March 8: Purchased 5,000 units from the local market at Sh.60 per unit.
  4. March 9: Sold 6,000 units at Sh.90 per unit. March 12: Purchased office furniture for Sh.40,000 for use in the business.
  5. March 15: Paid Sh.10,000 for photocopy and printing of office documents.
  6. March 18: Supplied 30,000 units to a department in the National Treasury at a price of Sh.85 per unit.
  7. March 20: Sold 2,500 units at Sh.90 per unit to a company in Uganda.
  8. March 23: Purchased on credit 2,500 units locally at Sh.80 per unit before deducting a cash discount of 5%.
  9. March 27: The directors' appropriated goods valued at Sh.320,000 which were not paid for.
  10. March 28: Paid electricity expense of Sh.15,000 and telephone expense of Sh.6,000.

All transactions were inclusive of VAT at the rate of 16% where applicable, unless otherwise specified.

Assume the rate of import duty is 20%.

Required

A value added tax (VAT) account for the month of March 2018 for Bandika Ltd. (10 marks)
